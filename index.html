Cidades

As duas principais cidades são Hanoi e Ho Chi Minh. Hanoi situa-se a norte do páis e Ho Chi Minh situa-se a sulé a capital e o centro cultural do Vietname, na qual passam os rios Vermelho e Duong. City é a maior cidade com uma importância na economia do país localizada no sul. Da Nang: A coastal city that serves as a major economic and transportation hub. Hai Phong: A large port city in northern Vietnam. Can Tho: The largest city in the Mekong Delta, known for its floating markets. Nha Trang: A popular beach resort city. Hue: An historic city that was the imperial capital of the Nguyen Dynasty.Ho Chi Minh Da Lat: A city in the Central Highlands known for its cooler climate and flowers. Hoi An: An ancient town and UNESCO World Heritage site.
